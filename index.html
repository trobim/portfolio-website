Product Designer

UX Design Lead - Digital Transformation - Strategy - Service Design - Digital Experience

I'am a lead UX Designer with over 10 years of experience designing and scaling user-centred digital experiences in complex environments.

My expertise spans UX strategy, service design, research, and design systems, with a focus on creating design cultures that empower teams to make data driven decisions.

At Assura, I lead UX strategy and design initiatives that simplify how hundreds of thousands of clients interact with their insurance services every day.

I collaborate across product, marketing, and IT to ensure every digital touchpoint feels consistent, accessible, and human.

Key strengths:

• UX & Service Design Strategy
• Design Systems & Scalable Processes
• Research-Driven Product Optimisation
• Stakeholder Alignment & Cross-Team Collaboration
• Team Mentorship & Design Operations

I’m passionate about making health insurance experiences simpler, clearer, and more trustworthy through thoughtful, data-driven design.

Fluent in English, French, and Portuguese.
